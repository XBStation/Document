Connect to Telemetry data¶

Comm Links -> choose TCP type

  • Host name/ip: 127.0.0.1 (default)
  • Remote port: 5760 (default)

Connect to Video Streaming¶

General -> Video -> choose video source: UDP Video Stream, UDP port: 5601
