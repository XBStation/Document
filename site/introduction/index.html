  • Introduction

XBStation is a solution for streaming fpv video and controling UAV via internet (wifi, 3G/4G/5G cellular network). it includes: open hardware on drone (PX4 and ArduPilot) and software on ground XBStation has been designed to provide the drone applications for delivery, surveys, security, ambulance and emergency response

Supported Flight Controllers¶

  • All supported Ardupilot boards including Pixhawk, APMx and Cube

Supported Companion Computers¶

  • Raspberry 2
  • Raspberry 3B/3B+
  • Raspberry 4
  • Odroid-XU4

Supported Cameras¶

  • CSI camera (ex: Raspberry camera V1, V2, ...)
  • USB camera (ex: Logitech C270, Logitech C920, ...)

Supported Ground Control Stations¶

  • QGroundControl
  • MissionPlanner
  • APM Planner
